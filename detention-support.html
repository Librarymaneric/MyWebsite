Family Safety Planning
3 Critical Steps

Step 1: Create a Contact and Financial Emergency Plan

  1. 24/7 Hotline: Call the Chicago/ICIRR hotline (1-855-435-7693)
  2. Attorney/Legal Aid: Contact your attorney or legal aid. Legal Support
  3. Trusted Ally: Identify at least two non-detained adults (friends, family, or neighbors) who know the plan and can be called immediately.
    • Financial Access: detail where important documents, keys, spare cash, and bank account information are located.
    • A-Number: Clearly write down the Alien Registration Number (A-Number) for every family member who has one. This is essential for locating a detained person quickly.
      • The A-Number must be provided exactly, including leading zeros (e.g., A012-345-678).

Step 2: Appoint a Standby Guardian (for Minor Children)


In Illinois, Standby Guardianship is a powerful tool allowing parents to designate a temporary guardian for their children without going to court until a specific event occurs, such as detention.
  1. What it does: Legally grants temporary physical care and control of your minor child(ren) to a trusted person if you are unable to care for them.
  2. Required Forms: Families must sign a Designation of Standby Guardian form, which must be witnessed.
  3. Actionable Advice: The designated guardian should have copies of the child's birth certificate, vaccination records, school information, and health insurance cards.

Step 3: Complete Power of Attorney


A Power of Attorney (POA) is a legal document that gives someone else (your designated agent) the authority to act on your behalf for specific tasks.
  1. Properties/Fianances (Durable POA): Allows your agent to access bank accounts, pay bills (mortgage, rent, utilities), and manage your property while you are detained. This ensures your home and finances are protected.
  2. Healthcare: Allows your agent to make medical decisions for you if you become incapacitated during an encounter or detention.
  3. Advice: The POA must be signed and notarized to be legally binding. Use these forms:
    • POA Property
    • POA Healthcare
